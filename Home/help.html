FAQs

AI-Driven Academic Advisor Platform

ADAAP is an AI-driven platform designed to assist students and advisors in academic planning by optimizing course selection, scheduling, and enrollment processes.

Students, advisors, university administrators, financial officers, and student affairs personnel can access and use the system.

Users can log in using their university credentials. If you experience issues, contact technical support.

How does ADAAP recommend courses?
The platform analyzes academic history, degree requirements, and student preferences to suggest the best courses for each semester.

Can I modify my recommended schedule?
Yes, students can make adjustments, but changes must comply with credit hour limits and prerequisite requirements.

What happens if a class is full?
ADAAP suggests alternative courses or notifies students when a seat becomes available.

How do I check my tuition and enrollment status?
Students can access their financial and enrollment details from the Tuition and Enrollment Management section.
